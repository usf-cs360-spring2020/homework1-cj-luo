Air Traffic Passenger statistics

San Francisco International Airport Report on Monthly Passenger Traffic Statistics by Airline. Airport data is seasonal in nature, therefore any comparative analyses should be done on a period-over-period basis (i.e. January 2010 vs. January 2009) as opposed to period-to-period (i.e. January 2010 vs. February 2010).

It is also important to note that fact and attribute field relationships are not always 1-to-1. For example, Passenger Counts belonging to United Airlines will appear in multiple attribute fields and are additive, which provides flexibility for the user to derive categorical Passenger Counts as desired

Data

Dataset has 21.2k and 12 colomns. Here is (the link) to the original dataset.

In addition to including a link to the source and license, you should describe the data. You might want a list of some sort to enumerate the different types of columns:

  1. Activity Period: the specific date of record. The range from 2005 to 2019
  2. GEO Region summary:catogory into different region, including US, Asia,Europe,Canada,Mexio,Australia/Oceania
  3. Published Airline: Give a description of Airline
  4. Passenger Count: Give a number of Passenger.

Visualizations

(including four static images from tableau and three visualizations creating by d3)

LineChart: The trend of sum of Passenger Count for Activity Period Month. Color shows details about Activity Period Year. The marks are labeled by Activity Period Year. The view is filtered on Activity Period Year, which keeps 2016, 2017 and 2018.

BarChart: Sum of Passenger Count for each GEO Region. The data is filtered on Activity Period Year, which keeps 2018.

BubbleChart: Color shows details about GEO Summary. Size shows sum of Passenger Count. The marks are labeled by Published Airline and sum of Passenger Count. The data is filtered on GEO Region, which keeps Asia and US.

PieChart: Color shows details about Operating Airline. Size shows sum of Passenger Count. The marks are labeled by Operating Airline and % of Total Passenger Count. The data is filtered on GEO Region, which keeps US.
